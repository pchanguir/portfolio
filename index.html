About me

Geology undergraduate at UFRJ (Federal University of Rio de Janeiro). Currently: a Scientific Initiation fellow at the Technological Characterization Sector of the Mineral Technology Center (SCT/CETEM) since August 2023; member of the database team of the AAPG-UFRJ Student Chapter since April 2024; teaching assistant for Structural Geology II since February 2024. In 2023, held a scholarship as a teaching assistant at the UFRJ Mineralogy Laboratory. Has experience working with the mineral cassiterite, programming languages such as Python and JavaScript, and analytical techniques such as Raman spectroscopy and scanning electron microscopy with energy-dispersive spectroscopy (SEM-EDS).

Main interests include: Mineralogical characterization of ores, Mineralogy, Geochemistry, Structural Geology, and Mineral Exploration.
